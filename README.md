## Solution

**SmoothPay = Income Smoothing Platform**

1. Connect your bank account
2. We calculate your 12-month average income
3. You get paid that amount every month
4. High months? Extra stays in account. Low months? We cover the gap.

**Result:** Steady paycheck. Reduced anxiety. Finally budget like a normal person.
